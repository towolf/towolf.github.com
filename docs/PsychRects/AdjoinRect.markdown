rect=AdjoinRect(rect,fixedRect,fixedSide)

Moves rect to be outside and alongside the specified edge of fixedRect.
The legal values for fixedSide are: RectLeft, RectRight,
RectTop, and RectBottom.
Also see PsychRects.
